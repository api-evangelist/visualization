For a while, somewhere in between 2007 and 2011, infographics — in the everyday persons sense, not the statisticians — were all the rage.They came in lots of shapes and sizes and covered a wide span of useful to useless topics.While these sort of graphics are still around, theyre dwarfed by a different brand of work these days.A more mature one.Mark Wilson for FastCompany describes the evolution.
