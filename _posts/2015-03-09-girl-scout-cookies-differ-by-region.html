The Girl Scouts, known for their annual cookie sales, uses two bakers.These two bakers make similar but different cookies, so Thin Mints in one area of the United States might not be the same as another.Gasp.Where you buy determines which ones you get.Orange County gets the crunchier Thin Mints, while Los Angeles gets the smoother ones; Dallas gets Samoas, but Fort Worth gets Caramel deLites.
