How CartoDB Powers Data Journalism From its beginning, CartoDB been a powerful tool for journalists because it’s easy, flexible, scalable and open source.With this simple-to-use tool journalists everywhere are discovering the immense power data visualization gives them for research and telling stories.Today more than 150 media organizations, many of the world’s most respected and widely-followed, use CartoDB in their online reporting.Journalist Alexandre Léchenet at lemonde.fr has used CartoDB to illustrate reports ranging from the housing travails of Roma groups in France to the different ways French cities have adjusted their students academic year.
